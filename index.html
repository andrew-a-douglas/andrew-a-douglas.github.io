About Me

Hi! I'm Andrew, usually known as a bagpipe teacher and performer, but lately honing my web development skills to see where that might lead.

Using WordPress, Keap (what used to be Infusionsoft), and a wide array of other web-plugins, I built a bagpipe educational website that I've run as my business for the last 10 years.
